    Yao Group Page

    We engage in the interaction between statistics and geometry

    We are a vibrant and innovative group situated within the Department of Statistics and Data Science.
    Composed of accomplished research fellows and motivated graduate students, under the guidance of a dedicated faculty member.
    Focus on the intersection of statistics and geometry in data science and explore its profound implications within the realm of authentic data science.
    Forged strong affiliations with our colleagues around the world, including leading experts in geometry, statistics, and various scientific disciplines.
